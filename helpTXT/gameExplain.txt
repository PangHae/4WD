This is the basic description of the game.

Players are the ones who have come to drift on a desert island.

Players are given 30 days. Surviving the island during this period, you must escape.

There are three ways to escape
1. Request for rescue from a ship or airplane due to smoke
2. Draw a stone sos and ask the plane for rescue
3. Build your own ship and escape

Escape the island in one of the ways above!
