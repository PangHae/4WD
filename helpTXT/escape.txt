A detailed explanation of how to escape.

1. rescue + parameter 
When requesting rescue on the ship by smoke, when requesting sos by plane
5% chance to succeed. When running, the requirements are as follows:

rescue
	action	hunger	thirst							
smoke	5	5	5	wood 30
sos	5	5	5	stone 30

2. escape

Escape directly with the crafted ship
There are three stages on the ship. 

In the case of a stage 1 ship, the chance of escape is 25%.
In the case of a stage 2 ship, the chance of escape is 50%.
In the case of a stage 3 ship, the chance of escape is 75%.
