The inventory shows key available resources.

Type of resource
1.water
2. meat
3. fruits
4. wood
5. stone
6. stoneSword
7. stoneAxe
8. stonePickAxe
9. ship
10. house

When consumed, water, meat, and fruits temporarily restore thirst and hunger levels.
Wood and stone are used to craft items for survival.
stoneSword, stoneAxe, and stonePickAxe are items for survival.
Ship is an item to escape from the island.
house is provided by default. There are levels in the house, and as you level up the house, your behavioral power increases.
